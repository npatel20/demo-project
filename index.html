who we are

We’re using nature’s most sustainable resources to create better products for us and for the planet.

Our brand was founded with this as a guiding principle. We don’t think it’s right to just pass the earth along from generation to generation. We think it’s time we started saving it for all the generations to come.

Our founders, Arvind Ganesan and Rahul Singh, formerly at Wayfair, are combining their deep knowledge of home goods, with their passion for sustainability, to transform the products we use every day. While EcoSoul was founded in Boston, we've now expanded our operations across the globe, with our supply chain spanning across India, China and Vietnam.
